The waterflow simulator shows the path of a waterdroplets / streams on a given surface type. This simulator was scripted as a part of my thesis, to help me find design variations which would allow the water to flow to each brick on my green fa√ßade. The idea behind the algorithm is quite simple: if the angle between the xy-plane and the plane perpendicular to the surface‚Äôs normal vector at a given point is between the critical movement angle and the dropping angle (which are characteristical to the given surface material), the droplet is moving.
